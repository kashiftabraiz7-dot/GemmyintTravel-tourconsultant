About Saudi Company Formation

Saudi Arabia has simplified foreign investment rules and offers multiple business structures. Popular options include Limited Liability Company (LLC), branch office, representative office and joint ventures. Key steps generally are: name reservation, MOA/articles, CR registration, obtaining licenses and visas, and registering with tax and labor authorities.
